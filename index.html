Our Story

A Legacy of Authentic Flavors

Since 2015, Kacchi Dine has been serving the most authentic Kacchi Biryani in Feni. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, preserving the traditional cooking methods that make our dishes truly special.

We use only the finest ingredients - premium basmati rice, hand-picked spices, and the freshest meat - to create dishes that honor our culinary heritage while delighting modern palates.
